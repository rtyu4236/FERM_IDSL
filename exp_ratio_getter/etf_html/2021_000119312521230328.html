The Fund seeks to track the investment results of the Dow Jones U.S. Select Telecommunications Index (the “Underlying Index”), which measures the performance of the telecommunications sector of the U.S. equity market, as defined by S&P Dow Jones Indices LLC (the “Index Provider” or “SPDJI”). mid-
