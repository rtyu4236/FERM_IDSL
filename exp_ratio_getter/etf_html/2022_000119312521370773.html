day-to-day
management of such Fund in addition to Mr. Whitehead. Information regarding the other Portfolio Managers for each Fund can be found in such Fund’s Summary Prospectus, Prospectus and SAI.

day-to-day
management of the Fund. Mr. Whitehead has been a Portfolio Manager of each Fund since 2022.
